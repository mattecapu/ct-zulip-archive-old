Topic: Explicit description of the lax monoidal functor classifier?

{% raw %}

Martti Karvonen (Jan 09 2022 at 17:56):

The famous BPK-paper guarantees that the inclusion of (strict) monoidal categories and strict monoidal functors into (strict) monoidal categories and lax monoidal functors has a left adjoint LLL. Hence the category of lax monoidal functors C→DC\to DC→D is isomorphic to that of strict monoidal functors L(C)→DL(C)\to DL(C)→D. Is there a known explicit construction of LLL in this case? For instance, I know that when 111 is the terminal monoidal category, then L(1)L(1)L(1) is the free monoidal category on a monoid, but I'd love to be able to compute L(−)L(-)L(−) more generally.
