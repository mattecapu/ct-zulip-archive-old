Fawzi Hreiki (Jul 04 2021 at 16:28):

Δ(F)\Delta(F)Δ(F) is just the constant diagram (i.e. functor) at FFF. A cone is a natural transformation from a constant diagram to a diagram. The statement that λ\lambdaλ is a limit means that λ\lambdaλ is the universal cone.
