Xuanrui Qi (Jul 31 2023 at 21:07):

Or, rather, think of it as a way how A relates to B! There is the standard category Rel\mathbf{Rel}Rel of sets and binary relations. Anotjer important example is from symplectic geometry: there is a notion called a Fukaya category, where (roughly) the objects are certain ("Lagrangian") submanifolds of a manifold and the morphisms are (very vaguely) the ways how those submanifolds intersect.
