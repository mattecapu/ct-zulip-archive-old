Steve Awodey (Apr 07 2023 at 16:29):

*** HoTT 2023
*** Second International Conference on Homotopy Type Theory
*** Carnegie Mellon University
*** 22nd-25th May 2023

Registration for the Second International Conference on Homotopy Type Theory is now open.
The conference will take place from the morning of Monday 22nd May to the evening of Thursday 25th May 2023 at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh.
You can register via the website
https://hott.github.io/HoTT-2023//registration/
The deadline for early registration is 1st May 2023.
The deadline for registration is 14th May 2023.
There is some NSF funding available to support participation of students,
which can cover registration, travel, and accommodation expenses.
To apply, please follow the instructions on the registration page.
With best regards,
Steve (on behalf of the Scientific and Organising Committees)
