On the Applied Category Theory discord server there is a Study Group using voice chat for meetings discussing Dr. Eugenia Cheng's Joy of Abstraction book in synchronization with the official book club at the Topos Institute. https://discord.gg/hTEpgYv https://topos.site/joa-bookclub/ It is on Fridays at 10 am PDT or 1 pm EDT. Please come join us if you are interested in reading this excellent beginners book on Category Theory with others. No previous mathematical experience required. This informal interactive study group is not sponsored by the Topos Institute.
