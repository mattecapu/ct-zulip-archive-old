Dan Marsden (Mar 28 2022 at 11:20):

The 2022 Structure meets Power workshop will take place on July 4th this year, co-located with ICALP in Paris.

The aim of the workshop is to bridge a divide in the field of logic in Computer Science, between two distinct strands: one focusing on semantics and compositionality (“Structure”), the other on expressiveness and complexity (“Power”). These two fundamental aspects of logic are studied using almost disjoint technical languages and methods, by almost disjoint research communities. We believe that bridging this divide is a major issue in Computer Science, and may hold the key to fundamental advances in the field.

From the perspective of the this community, category theory is a fundamental component of the "Structure" component of the workshop. Typically the "Power" component might be represented by (finite) model theory, combinatorics, complexity theory and the study of algorithms.

The deadline for submissions is 20th May 2022. Details can be found in the call for papers:

https://easychair.org/cfp/smp-2022

The workshop will be run as a hybrid event, although on site participation is highly recommended.
