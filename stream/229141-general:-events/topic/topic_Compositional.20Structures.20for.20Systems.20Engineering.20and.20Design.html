Topic: Compositional Structures for Systems Engineering and Design

{% raw %}

John Baez (Oct 18 2022 at 17:50):

There will be a workshop at the National Institute of Standards and Technology November 3-4 2022:

  • Compositional Structures for Systems Engineering and Design.

The focus of this workshop is to foster the development of applications of category theory to systems engineering and design. We are interested in bringing together experts from engineering and applied category theory, and those involved in software tools development. There will be several talks/demos showcasing existing approaches as well as ample time for discussion.

It's being organized by Joseph Moeller and Priyaa Varshinee Srinivasan, and here are the currently confirmed speakers:

  • Angeline Aguinaldo (University of Maryland / Applied Physics Lab)
  • Georgios Bakirtzis (University of Texas, Austin)
  • Zinovy Diskin (University of Waterloo)
  • Fabrizio Genovese (20squares)
  • Jules Hedges (University of Strathclyde)
  • Alan Hylton (NASA)
  • Pascal Le Masson (MINES PariTech)
  • Esteban Montero (Chevron)
  • Evan Patterson (Topos Institute)
  • David Perner (University of Alabama)
  • Pawel Sobocinski (Tallinn University of Technology)
  • David Spivak (Topos Institute)
  • Donald Thompson (Microsoft)
  • Derek Wise (Lockheed Martin)
  • Ryan Wisnesky (Conexus)
