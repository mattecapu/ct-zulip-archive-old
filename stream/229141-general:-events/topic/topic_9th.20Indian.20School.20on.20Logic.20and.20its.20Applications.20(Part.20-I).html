9th Indian School on Logic and its Applications, ISLA(Part-I), A Virtual School

https://sites.google.com/view/isla-2022-iitk/home
(May 16-21, 2022)

On behalf of the Association for Logic in India (ALI), the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK) is pleased to organize 9th Indian School on Logic and its Applications (Part-I) in virtual mode. The theme of the school will be Logic and Dualities, and the lectures are roughly divided into streams, namely Stone-type dualities and categorical syntax-semantics dualities. The school is open to participants interested in mathematics, computer science, philosophy, linguistics and other areas from around the world.

ISLA is a biennial event of the Association of Logic in India (ALI). The details of the last edition of the school can be found here.
