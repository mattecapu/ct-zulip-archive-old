Dear all,

ItaCa Fest is back again, and ready for 2023! As in the previous editions, the Fest collects a wide range of topics and gives voice to a large number of communities.

The first date of the ItaCa Fest will be April 27, 2023 at 3 pm (Italian time) featuring:

15:00 - Tobias Fritz,
15:40 - Elena Di Lavore
16:20 - Davide Trotta

The zoom link is the following: https://stockholmuniversity.zoom.us/j/68792232558
While the Fest website is this one: https://progetto-itaca.github.io/pages/fest22.html

The Fest will continue with other meetings in May, June, September, October and November, with other amazing speakers, such as Awodey, Escardo, Freni, Gambino, Henry, Koudenburg, Menni, Guffanti, Wrigley, Volpe.

Join us (and bring a friend)!

Cheers,
Beppe, Ivan, Fosco, Paolo and Greta.
