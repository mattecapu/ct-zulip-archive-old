Mike Shulman (Nov 08 2022 at 16:49):

We're talking about thinking of profunctors as morphisms between categories. So if HHH is a morphism from AAA to BBB, you can imagine "composing" it with g:B→Dg:B\to Dg:B→D even though they're different kinds of morphisms.
