Topic: Heat Bath in Markov Chains

{% raw %}

Owen Lynch (Nov 12 2021 at 15:13):

There is an an algorithm for sampling from a canonical distribution which makes a Markov chain that has the canonical distribution as its limit, and just runs that Markov chain for a while. States in this Markov chain can go up or down in energy, which seems to me like the Markov chain is precisely the system attached to a heat bath.
