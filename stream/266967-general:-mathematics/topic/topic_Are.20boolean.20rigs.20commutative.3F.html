Seems a bit strange to call that a Boolean rig; "idempotent rig" seems more transparent to me.
Observe that the idempotent condition results in xyxy=xxyyxyxy = xxyyxyxy=xxyy; this is close enough to commutativity that one can see how it might collapse to commutativity in a bunch of special cases.
