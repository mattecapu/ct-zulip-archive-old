JS PL (he/him) (Mar 23 2023 at 23:56):

But here's an attempt at an answer:
Let CRING\mathsf{CRING}CRING be the category of commutative (unital) rings. And let P:CRING→CRINGP: \mathsf{CRING} \to \mathsf{CRING}P:CRING→CRING be the functor which maps P(A)=A[X]P(A) = A[X]P(A)=A[X].

JS PL (he/him) (Mar 23 2023 at 23:57):

Alternatively, we may write this as P(A)=A⊗Z[X]P(A) = A \otimes \mathbb{Z}[X]P(A)=A⊗Z[X] -- where the tensor product of Z\mathbb{Z}Z-modules, which is the coproduct in CRING\mathsf{CRING}CRING
