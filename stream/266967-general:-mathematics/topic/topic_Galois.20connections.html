John Baez (Apr 20 2023 at 00:20):

A bunch of them are really best thought of as contravariant adjunctions between interesting categories, while some are contravariant adjunctions between mere posets.

John Baez (Apr 20 2023 at 00:24):

Furthermore, 3 of them are dualities between commutative rings and spaces, where we get a commutative ring on a space from looking at ring-valued functions on that space. 2 of them are dualities between abelian groups of some kind and abelian groups of the same kind, obtained by homming the group into the circle group. And 2 are dualities between 'covering spaces' and their groups of deck transformations.

John Baez (Apr 20 2023 at 00:25):

So I'd say there are at most 3 basic ideas kinds of Galois connections listed here... each of which can be worked out in many different ways.
