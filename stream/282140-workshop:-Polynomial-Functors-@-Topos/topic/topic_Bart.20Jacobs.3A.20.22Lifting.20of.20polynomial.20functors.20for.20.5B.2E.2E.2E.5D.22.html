Abstract:
This will be a tutorial talk about explaining how logical reasoning principles associated with (co)algebras of a polynomial functor, as data types, can be obtained, via lifting of the functor to a logical level. This lifting happens in the context of fibrations, as categorical logical models. The lifting exploits the polynomial structure.

Original source: C. Hermida and B. Jacobs, Structural induction and coinduction in a fibrational setting, Information and Computation 145 (1998), p.107-152.
