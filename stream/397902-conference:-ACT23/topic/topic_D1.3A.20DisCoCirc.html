A question was mentioned about a comonoid interpretation in these string diagrams, and I wanted to add on by asking if there's a reason we'd want to use something like the -self resolving frame in the talk instead of a coproduct?

Vincent Wang (Jul 31 2023 at 16:17):

because you might not have coproducts around to do your interpretation; all you really know is that you've got some method "-self" that sends your binary gates to unary gates.

that being said, if you do have coproducts around then there's nothing stopping you from using those! there's a lot of other options depending on what the monoidal category for semantics has lying around.
