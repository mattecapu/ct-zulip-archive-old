Spencer Breiner (Jul 29 2023 at 01:09):

For anyone taking public transit to College Park, the Green line train is out of service for the summer.

It has been replaced by free bus service from the Fort Totten metro station. There are two bus lines running, so make sure to take the express to College Park/Greenbelt, not the one to Hyattsville.

There is also a free shuttle for the ~1 km from the College Park metro/bus drop off to the university campus (104 bus line), but it only runs M-F. Check online for weekend lines or try Lyft/Uber for the last leg.
