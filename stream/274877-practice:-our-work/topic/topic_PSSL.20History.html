Valeria de Paiva (Oct 15 2022 at 04:47):

Another small bit of celebration for Dana Scott's 90th Birthday: the Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic (PSSL) is an informal meeting of categorists, type theorists and logicians, started in 1976 by Dana Scott, while in Oxford. There's now a nLab page for it, but we only have the titles of the talks till the 100th PSSL. We're missing the last 5 meetings and help providing webpages or listings of the talks for these missing meetings would be much appreciated!
