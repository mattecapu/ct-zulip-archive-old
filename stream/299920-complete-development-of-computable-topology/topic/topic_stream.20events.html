Graham Manuell (Sep 08 2021 at 16:42):

There are many different approaches to computable topology and each will have a different definition of space. To me one of the more straightforward definitions would be a locale internal to some realisability topos. You can also easily find Paul Taylor's definition in his work on Abstract Stone Duality. Both of these will satisfy your criterion. There are also more spatial approaches which do not.
