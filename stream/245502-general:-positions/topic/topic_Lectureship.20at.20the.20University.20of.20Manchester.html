The Department of Mathematics of the University of Manchester is advertising a Lectureship in Pure Mathematics, with Logic explicitly mentioned as one of the possible areas for the appointment. For those unfamiliar with the UK system, a lectureship is a permanent position, involving research, teaching and administration. I think it is somewhat comparable to an Assistant Professorship in the USA.

Further details can be found at this web page. The deadline for applications is 2nd November 2023. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.
