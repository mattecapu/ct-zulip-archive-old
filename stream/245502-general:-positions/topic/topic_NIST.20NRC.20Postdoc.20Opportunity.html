Spencer Breiner (Jun 16 2022 at 13:03):

The Applied Category Theory group at NIST (US National Institute of Standards and Technology) would like to encourage applications for the National Research Council's Research Associates Program (NRC RAP). Applications are due August 1, to begin in the first half of 2023.

Our group is currently advertising two post-doctoral opportunities focused on Mathematical Foundations for System Interoperability and Research in Cyber-Physical Systems. Program information like eligibility, stipend, application instructions and FAQ can be found at the RAP website. Unfortunately, these positions are only open to US citizens.

NRC applicants are encouraged to contact target institutions well before the application deadline. If you are interested, first read through the general application instructions and the specific research opportunities linked above. Then write up a 1-2 page sketch of potential research directions and send it to me at spencer.breiner@nist.gov.

Feel free to post here or DM me with any questions.
