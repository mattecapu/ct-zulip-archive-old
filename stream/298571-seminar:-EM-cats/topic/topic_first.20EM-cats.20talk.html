Today the Topos Institute started its newest seminar series, the EM-cats with a talk by @Jade Master on
"The Universal Property of the Algebraic Path Problem".

The Emerging Researchers in Category Theory (Em-Cats) Virtual Seminar is a public series of virtual seminars, given by graduate students in Category Theory around the world. Eugenia Cheng will assist each speaker in the preparation of their talk, to ensure that all talks are excellent. As we say on the webpage.

Check the program at https://topos.site/em-cats/ and check Jade's stellar talk (with chairing by Martin Hyland) at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=inH26ggKJfc.
